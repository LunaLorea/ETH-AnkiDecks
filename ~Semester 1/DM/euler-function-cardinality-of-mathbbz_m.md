## Back
\(\varphi(m)=|\mathbb{Z}^*_m|=\prod\limits^r_{i=0}(p_i-1)p_i^{e_{i}-1}\) where \(e_i\) is the exponent from the prime factorization of m. If m is prime trivially \(\varphi(p)=p-1\).
